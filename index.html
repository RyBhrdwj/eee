IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • BRANCH UPGRADATION

    Branch upgradation depends on number of vacant seats left in the desired branch. Minimum target to get branch upgradation is 9.0 GPA in both the semesters.
    Since it is undecided whether the marks of only the 1st Sem will be considered or of 1st Year.
    Upgradation to ECE is relatively easier compared to CSE/IT.

  • MUST REVISE TOPICS FROM 11th/12th

    These topics serve as foundation for chapters in harder subjects like AM, AP, ES etc.
    Without having a grasp on these topics, it's difficult to understand what's being taught in the classes. A list for these topics is given below :

    • Differentiation, Integration
    • Vector
    • Matrices
    • Laws and Formulas covered in Electromagnetism

  • CCA/MENTORSHIP/LIBRARY ?

    These periods are dedicated for Co-Curricular activities and NO ATTENDANCE is taken.
    These periods are treated as free periods and Saturday is treated as an OFF DAY.

  • ATTENDANCE

    Depends from teacher to teacher and while BPIT isn't strict about attendance, it is advisable to maintain 75% attendance in the first year.
    So as to avoid backlog, get full marks for attendance and study as much as possible in college itself.

  • EXAMS

    Mid Sem will be conducted roughly around first week of January.
    End Sem will be conducted roughly around first week of March.
